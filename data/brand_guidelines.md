## Brand Voice & Tone

### Personality
- **Authoritative but approachable** — we cite real science, but translate it for a smart non-academic audience
- **Optimistic and forward-looking** — longevity is exciting, not scary
- **Direct and no-fluff** — get to the point fast; respect your audience's intelligence
- **Slightly edgy** — we talk about things mainstream health accounts avoid

### Writing Style
- Short punchy sentences for hooks and slide 1
- Use numbers: "3 peptides," "72 hours," "87% reduction"
- Active voice always
- No emojis in body copy (save for CTA only, max 1–2)
- Avoid: "game-changer," "revolutionary," "hack" (overused)
- Preferred terms: "protocol," "stack," "mechanism," "evidence-based," "longevity optimization"

### Content Pillars
1. **Research Breakdowns** — translate a new study into actionable takeaways
2. **Protocol Deep Dives** — specific peptide dosing, timing, synergies
3. **Myth Busting** — correct common misconceptions with citations
4. **Biohacker Profiles** — what top longevity researchers actually do
5. **Mechanism Explainers** — how a peptide/pathway actually works (mTOR, GH axis, etc.)

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Primary:** Deep navy `#0A1628`
- **Accent:** Electric teal `#00D4FF`
- **Secondary accent:** Warm gold `#FFB347`
- **Text on dark:** White `#FFFFFF`
- **Text on light:** Near-black `#1A1A2E`
- **Background option:** Dark gradient `#0A1628 → #1A1A2E`

### Typography
- **Headlines/hooks:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat Bold or similar)
- **Body copy:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- **Citations/footnotes:** Italic, smaller size

### Image Style
- Dark backgrounds preferred
- Molecular/cellular imagery
- No stock photo "gym bro" aesthetics
- Clean data visualizations welcome
